WHAT IS
LAforant ?
Laforant is a colorful first person shooter that expands upon team-based action gameplay. Build a team with our diverse cast of agents and win the matches in exhilarating 5v5 combat ! Players play as one of a set of agents that are designed based on several countries and cultures around the world. In the main game mode, players are assigned to either the attacking or defending team with each team having five players on it. Each agents have unique abilities that can change the tide of the match. Choose your agent, build a team, and win the game.
